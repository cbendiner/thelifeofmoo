My Name is Maddie Moo

Welcome to my page! My name is Maddie Moo and I am 12 years old. I was welcomed into the Bendiner home in 2009 when I was just a puppy. My mom found me on the way to pick up my human siblings from work. My dog siblings and I were being given away for free. I ran right up to my new owner and knew this is the human mom I want to be with. I am a border collie bundle of joy. My favorite snacks are marshmallows, cheese, and chicken. At the start of 2020, I have started a growing a cyst on my stomach that gets bigger and bigger everyday. Later this year, I have a risky surgery scheduled. My family just wants me to know how much I'm loved.
